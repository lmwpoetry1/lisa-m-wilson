Language Liberation & Literacy Consulting

Language is more than communication. It is identity, memory, culture, intelligence, connection, and possibility.

Lisa's work supports organizations, educators, systems leaders, and communities in reimagining literacy and language development through affirming, human-centered, and culturally sustaining approaches.
